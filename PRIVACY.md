# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

knockoffwatch does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.

## What stays on your device

| Data | Where it lives |
|---|---|
| Health readings (heart rate, blood pressure, blood oxygen) | Written to Apple Health on your iPhone only |
| 30-day reading history | Stored in the app's private Documents folder on your device |
| Watch pairing preference | Stored in your device's local UserDefaults |

## What the app does not do

- No advertising or tracking
- No network requests of any kind outside of Apple's required creash reporting
- No account, login, or cloud sync other than apple health
- No data shared with third parties

## Bluetooth

The app uses Bluetooth solely to communicate with your paired watch. No Bluetooth data is logged, transmitted, or stored beyond what is needed to display a reading.

## Apple Health

Health readings are written to Apple Health only if you grant permission during onboarding. You can revoke that permission at any time in **Settings → Privacy & Security → Health**. Revoking permission does not delete readings already written to Apple Health.

## Contact

This is a personal open-source project. Questions or concerns can be raised via [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/fakewatch/knockoffwatch/issues).
